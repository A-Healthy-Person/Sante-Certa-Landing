Community Guidelines

We built Santé Certa for respectful, purpose-driven contact. Submissions are moderated.

By using this site, you agree to the guidelines below.

What’s not allowed

  • Harassment or intimidation — including persistent or unwanted contact.
  • Stalking or surveillance behaviors — attempts to track, locate, or monitor any person.
  • Impersonation or misrepresentation — pretending to be someone else or speaking on their behalf.
  • Doxxing or privacy violations — sharing personal identifiers, medical details of others, or confidential information.
  • Sexual, violent, or hateful content, or anything meant to threaten or degrade.
  • Circumventing safety measures — including filters, rate limits, or moderation.

Use of the contact form

  • Keep messages brief and relevant to collaboration, product, or support.
  • Don’t include links, emails, phone numbers, or files; off-platform contact requests may be ignored.
  • Avoid posting anyone else’s personal or medical information.

Moderation & consequences

  • We may decline, delete, or ignore submissions at our discretion.
  • Serious or repeat misuse may result in permanent blocking and, if warranted, referral to the appropriate authorities.
  • We minimize data collection, but we may retain limited security logs to protect the service.

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.
